Back-end development

A project needs a strong foundation and an architecture that is flexible. Our back-ends are easy to deploy and maintain as we make use of IT automation tools such as Docker, Puppet and Vagrant. We build secure and scalable systems using a strong Open Source software foundation. It is our belief that there is no silver bullet in software and thus we offer expertise in a wide variety of technologies.

Front-end development

A strong back-end will be worth way more with a matching front-end. To achieve the best results we employ modern frameworks such as AngularJS and Bootstrap. But what is beauty without smarts? Our apps are not just pretty but also reliable- testing is really important to us and front-ends are not an exception.

Mobile development

We can develop mobile applications on top of our reliable back-ends. We'll be excited to create iOS or Android apps for projects that we see have great potential.

Experience design

Design is at the core of our products. We want to develop modern software with intuitive interfaces that are a joy to use. We'll be happy to develop concepts together, responsive interfaces, mobile designs or even help with your brand image.
