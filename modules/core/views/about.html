We are a software development company that started its voyage in 2013. Since then we've been busy baking awesome software for companies from Aarhus and Copenhagen.

Background

We're a team of hackers, automation freaks and console cowboys who like to work with Open Source software and to apply it in both Open and Commercial projects. Our expertise spans a wide variety of IT subjects such as Internet of Things, Distributed Systems, Open Data, IT Automation, Security, IP Telephony, Front-end design and more. We build beautiful and intelligent Web systems and by doing so aim to contribute to building Web 3.0.

Our culture

We invest plenty of time into sending the right message. Here at fadeit, we have a different perspective on work. We want our projects to be enjoyable and rewarding, not only for us, but for you too. We want to grow together and build awesome software that'll make you and us happy for a long, long time.
