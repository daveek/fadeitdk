We are a software development company that started its voyage in 2013. Since then we've been busy baking awesome software for companies from Aarhus and Copenhagen.

Our culture

Here at fadeit, we try to have a different perspective on work. We want our projects to be enjoyable and rewarding, not only for us, but for our clients too. We want to grow together and build awesome software that'll make you and us happy for a long, long time.

We like to have fun as much as we like to be serious. One of fadeit's founding principles is to have fun while being part of it, no matter what fun means to you. It could be racing your chair, coding, drinking a beer or doing all at once.
